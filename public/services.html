Cash Pay Rates

$250 for initial 90 minute intake
$150 for 50 min individual or family therapy sessions


Cancelation/No Show Policy

Any session canceled more than 24 hours before will not be charged. Any late (less than 24 hours) or no show will be charged full session rate of $150.


Late Fee Policy

For clients using insurance, arriving late (11 minutes or more) to a scheduled session will incur a $50 late fee. Self-pay clients arriving late to session will be charged the full session amount with no additional late fee. Regardless of payment, an appointment is considered a no-show if client has not arrived by 20 minutes after the hour.


RPT Supervision


Therapists working toward a credential as a Registered Play Therapist through the Association of Play Therapy are required to obtain a minimum of 35 hours of play therapy supervision from a Registered Play Therapist Supervisor. I offer 60 minute RPT supervision sessions in person or via telehealth.

Cash Rate - $150
